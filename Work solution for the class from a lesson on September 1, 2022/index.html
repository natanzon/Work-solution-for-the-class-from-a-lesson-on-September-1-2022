Pancakes recipe

A simple and easy recipe for meking Pancakes that children love


ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • pinch salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

preparation

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make a hole in it and add the milk, the beaten egg, the oil and the vanilla extract.
  3. Stir with a whisk until there are no lumps, wait 5 minutes.
  4. Pour the mixture using a ladle into a greased Teflon pan.
  5. Fry on medium heat until bubbles form on the pancake, then carefully turn over to the other side and fry for another minute.
  6. Continue like this with the rest of the mixture, oiling lightly from time to time between frying.
  7. Serve with maple sauce, powdered sugar, or any toppings you like.
